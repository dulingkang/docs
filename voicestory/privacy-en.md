# VoiceStory Privacy Policy (English)

[简体中文](privacy)

VoiceStory (“we” / “the app”) respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data we collect

### On-device data (stored only on your device)

- **Playback data**: progress, recently played, completion status, and similar
- **Favorites and preferences**: favorites list, continuous-play setting, playback speed, and similar
- **Subscription status**: whether you are a subscriber (handled and provided by Apple)

This data is used mainly to provide the listening experience and is generally stored only on your device.

### Data we do not collect

- Your name, email, phone number, or other directly identifying information (unless you contact us by email on your own initiative)
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

---

## 2. Third-party services

### Apple (App Store and subscriptions)

- Purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple
- We do not obtain or store your payment information
- Apple may process related information under its own policies; please refer to Apple’s privacy policy

---

## 3. Children’s privacy

VoiceStory offers content suitable for children, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require you to create an account.

---

## 4. Your rights

- **View and manage**: you can review history and settings inside the app
- **Deletion**: uninstalling the app removes related data stored on the device

---

## 5. Policy updates

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will publish the changes on this page.
