# Capture Privacy Policy

Capture ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Overview

Capture is a photo and video editing app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure. Most of your data stays on your device.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### Data stored on your device (local only)

- **Photos and videos**: Your media files are processed on your device
- **Settings**: Preferences for filters, beauty effects, and app configuration
- **Subscription status**: Whether you have Premium (handled by Apple)

This data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. It is not sent to our servers unless you use cloud features.

### Data we do NOT collect

- Name, email, or other personally identifiable information (unless you provide it for support)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Browsing or usage analytics

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

### Apple (App Store & Subscriptions)

- Purchase and subscription management is handled by Apple
- Apple may collect payment and account information per their [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- We do not receive or store your payment details

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

- To provide photo editing, filters, and beauty effects
- To manage your subscription status
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Media and settings data are stored locally using Apple frameworks
- Cloud storage (Premium) is optional; data is transmitted only when you use cloud features
- Deleting the app removes all local data

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Capture does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require an account or registration for basic use.

---

## 7. Your Rights

- **Access**: Your data is stored on your device; you can manage it in the app
- **Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- **Opt-out**: Do not use cloud features to avoid external data transmission

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
