## FAQ

### Subscription

**Q: How do I subscribe to Premium?**
- Open the App → Profile → Subscription/Premium → Choose monthly or yearly plan to purchase.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase?**
- Sign in with the same Apple ID and it will restore automatically, or tap "Restore Purchase" on the subscription page.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
- Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Select BreathAI → Cancel Subscription.

**Q: What are the limits for free users?**
- Free users can do 2 breathing sessions per day. Premium members enjoy unlimited sessions plus AI-powered personalized insights.

---

### Features

**Q: How do I turn music and sound effects on/off?**
- Profile → Advanced Settings → Toggle background music and breathing sound effects, adjust volume.

**Q: Where are my practice records stored?**
- All practice data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud. Your privacy is protected.

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
- iPhone and iPad, iOS 16.2 or later.

---

### Technical Support

**Q: The app crashes. What should I do?**
- Try force-quitting the app and reopening it, or restart your device. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

**Q: AI insights are not working?**
- AI features require an internet connection and a Premium subscription. Check your network and subscription status.
