# BreathAI Privacy Policy

BreathAI ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Overview

BreathAI is a breathing exercise app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure. Most of your data stays on your device.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### Data stored on your device (local only)

- **Practice records**: duration, date, and how you felt after each session (e.g., relaxed, calm, focused)
- **Settings**: preferred duration, music volume, notification preferences
- **Subscription status**: whether you have Premium (handled by Apple)

This data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. It is not sent to our servers.

### Data we do NOT collect

- Name, email, or other personally identifiable information
- Location data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Browsing or usage analytics

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

### Apple (App Store & Subscriptions)

- Purchase and subscription management is handled by Apple
- Apple may collect payment and account information per their [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- We do not receive or store your payment details

### OpenAI (AI Features)

- Premium users can access AI-powered breathing insights
- When you use this feature, we send only the data needed to generate suggestions (e.g., practice summary) to the AI service
- This data is processed according to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- We do not store the content of AI responses on our servers

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

- To provide breathing exercises, history, and statistics
- To personalize AI insights (for Premium users)
- To manage your subscription status
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Practice and settings data is stored locally on your device using Apple frameworks (Core Data, UserDefaults)
- Data is only transmitted when you use AI features (Premium)
- We do not operate our own servers that store your practice data
- Deleting the app removes all local data

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

BreathAI is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require an account or registration.

---

## 7. Your Rights

- **Access**: Your practice data is stored on your device; you can view it in the app
- **Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- **Correction**: You can edit or clear your records within the app
- **Opt-out of AI**: Do not use AI features; all other features work without sending data externally

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes may also be communicated via app update notes.
