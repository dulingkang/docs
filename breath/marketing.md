# BreathAI

**Your personal breathing coach. Breathe. Relax. Focus.**

BreathAI helps you improve physical and mental wellness through guided breathing exercises. Whether you want to relieve stress, sleep better, or sharpen focus—BreathAI is your go-to app.

---

## Why BreathAI?

- **Guided breathing** — Visual breathing ball, real-time countdown, clear inhale/exhale cues
- **Music & sounds** — Soothing background music and breathing sound effects
- **Practice history** — Automatic logging, statistics, and progress tracking
- **Privacy first** — All data stored locally on your device
- **Works offline** — Practice anywhere, anytime (core features)
